A small collection of projects I've done with C#, MVC, HTML/CSS and more.

Tuscany Villa

Tuscany Villa is project showing my best attempt at replicating a mockup website. I needed to mimick the images used in the jumbotron, recreate the grid which offered both images and amenities of the hotel. At the bottom of this website there's a section for guests to leave messages to the hotel (Tuscany Villa) and I struggled with getting the comment box to fit in the right 6 rows of the screen.

Skills used:
HTML | CSS | Bootstrap

Organize Your Wardrobe

This website was designed to allow users to virtually organize their closet by category, color, season and occasion. Through database first programming users were given a list of seasons, colors, occassions and types of clothing to chose from when entering their clothes. In addition, users are given the ability to add their own special occassion that isn't listed in the given set. Users are also able to edit and delete items they've added to their closet.

Skills used:
Database first MVC

Bank Account

The Bank Account console application was built mimick what it's like visiting an ATM. Users are able to choose which account they want to view and also whether they want to deposit or withdraw money from their chosen account.

Skills used:
OOP | Inheritence and Polymorphism

A simple website for the popular video game Life Is Strange. The website showcases a trailer video for the game that is visible right at the start up of the page. Towards the bottom three lorem ipsum thumbnails also give sneak peeks into the first three episodes of Life is Strange.
