The Fortune Teller!

This was the first fully functional program I had ever written. It's a simple console application that asks users a series of questions and
gives them a fortune based on their answers.

The Lucky Lotto!

This program was designed to allow users to choose two numbers for a range set and then guess six numbers inside that range.
This project was particularly difficult for me because I had to figure out how to get the computer to randomly generate 6 numbers
and then tell the user how much money they won based on how many numbers they guessed correctly.

Safe Havens in The Last of Us

My review site for safe havens in The Last of Us was one of my favorite projects to work on because it was the most difficult for me.
After getting it functional and responsive, it was fun to play with the design. Though it was one of the most difficult it was also one
of the most fun because I made this project based around my favorite video game.

Who Am I?

I was first introduced to the world of software development in my highschool robotics class. Shortly after I was introduced to The Last of Us which showed me a different side of coding that I had never known about. I was fascinated by every aspect of software development and what could be made possible through coding. It is challenging and mentally stimulating and since my initial introduction to it I knew that I wanted to have a career in software development.
